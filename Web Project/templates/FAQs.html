FAQs

Features of online Tutoring

There are interactive courseware, collaborative blackboard, and rich forms of student-teacher interaction in the classroom. Online help is available at any time if you encounter problems. After the live class, you can also view the class replay and review the content of the class.

Yes, you will need a microphone and a webcam so that you can communicate directly with the tutor.

Once the lesson starts, the system will automatically record the sound and lesson content during the lesson, and you can view the playback on the client after the lesson is over. Don’t worry about your privacy, your video picture will not be visible.

If you encounter any problems during the lesson, you can Click on the ‘Online help’ button in the upper right corner of the classroom. After you describe your question, our technical support staff will establish a dialogue with you instantly to solve your problem.

Technical Questions

Please uninstall the old version first.

Please try to restart the client and log in again.

Before using Online-Class, please close Zoom, Google Meet and other meeting programs. Then perform device detection. The camera can only be seen when both parties enter the classroom. Please re-plug the external device and check if it is connected. Refresh the classroom afterwards. If you still can't see it during the class, please check if you are using the correct client.
